What is Sigmetic?

Sigmetic is an AI-powered Review Management & Business Intelligence Tool that helps businesses collect, analyze, and act on customer feedback. It transforms raw reviews into actionable insights to improve customer satisfaction, increase brand reputation, and drive business growth.

Designed for Traditional Businesses

Sigmetic is built specifically for traditional businesses such as restaurants, salons, local stores, and service providers who need a simple but powerful way to manage feedback without technical complexity.
